Figure requirements for Plos Computational Biology

Format: .tif
Size: under 10 MB
Dimensions: max 7.5 in W * 8.75 in H
	    min 2.63 in W
Color: RGB (8 bit/channel) or greyscale
Text within: Arial, Times, or Symbol font, 8-12 point
Lines: min 0.5 point
Resolution: 300-600 dpi
Compression: LZW
Background color: white


What we use:
RGB, 600 dpi

All can be modified in Visio or Python.
